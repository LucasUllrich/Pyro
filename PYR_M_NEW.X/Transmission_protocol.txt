Byte Zero:
    Address of Receiver
        0xFF: Master
        0x00: Slave 1
        0x01: Slave 2
        0x02: Slave 3

First Byte:
    Transmitter
        0xFF: Master
        0x00: Slave 1
        0x01: Slave 2
        0x02: Slave 3

Second Byte:
    Performed Operation
        'I': Ignite
        'T': Test connection

Third Byte:
    Adressed port of Slave
        0x00: Port 1
        0x01: Port 2
        .
        .
        0x09: Port 10

Fourth Byte: Reception by Master only
    Status of tested pin
        0x00: no connection
        0x01: connected
